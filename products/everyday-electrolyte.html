Everyday Electrolyte

Product Overview

Everyday Electrolyte is a comprehensive daily hydration and mineral balance supplement. This perfectly balanced formula replaces essential electrolytes (sodium, potassium, calcium, and magnesium) lost through normal activity, sweat, and everyday metabolic processes.

How It Works for Your Horse

The electrolyte formula maintains optimal hydration and mineral balance by:

  • Restoring essential minerals lost daily
  • Supporting proper hydration status
  • Maintaining cellular function
  • Supporting muscle contraction
  • Promoting healthy nerve function

Key Benefits

  • Perfect balance of essential electrolytes
  • Encourages water intake and hydration
  • Supports daily performance and endurance
  • Ideal during warm weather or work
  • Palatable flavor horses enjoy
  • Safe for daily use

Usage Instructions

Add to drinking water or feed daily. Ensure fresh water is always available. Particularly beneficial during warm weather, intense activity, or travel.

Ideal For

All horses, especially those in active work, during warm weather, long-distance travel, or competition. Perfect for maintaining baseline hydration and electrolyte balance.
