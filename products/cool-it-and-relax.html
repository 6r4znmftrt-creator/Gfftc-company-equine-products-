Cool It and Relax

Product Overview

Cool It and Relax is a specialized calming supplement formulated to support emotional balance and behavioral stability in horses. This premium blend combines natural calming ingredients proven to reduce anxiety and promote relaxation without compromising performance or alertness.

How It Works for Your Horse

The carefully selected ingredients work to naturally calm nervous and anxious horses by:

  • Supporting neurotransmitter balance
  • Reducing stress-related behaviors
  • Promoting mental clarity and focus
  • Supporting healthy cortisol levels
  • Encouraging natural relaxation responses

Key Benefits

  • Ideal for nervous, spooky, or anxious horses
  • Helps during stressful situations (shows, travel, trailer loading)
  • Non-sedating - maintains performance capability
  • Natural ingredients with no harmful side effects
  • Supports consistent behavioral patterns
  • Works within 30-45 minutes of administration

Usage Instructions

Administer 30-45 minutes before stressful situations or daily for chronic anxiety. Mix with feed or as directed. Consult your veterinarian for specific dosing.

Ideal For

Nervous horses, competition animals requiring behavioral support, traveling horses, young or inexperienced horses, and any horse showing signs of stress or anxiety.
