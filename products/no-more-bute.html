No More Bute

Product Overview

No More Bute is a natural pain and inflammation relief supplement designed as a safe alternative to conventional NSAIDs (Non-Steroidal Anti-Inflammatory Drugs). This powerful blend combines proven herbal and natural ingredients to support joint comfort and mobility without the risks associated with long-term pharmaceutical use.

How It Works for Your Horse

The synergistic formula works to naturally manage pain and inflammation through:

  • Powerful anti-inflammatory action
  • Natural pain relief mechanisms
  • Support for joint function and mobility
  • Reduction of stiffness and lameness
  • Support for soft tissue recovery

Key Benefits

  • Natural alternative to pharmaceutical painkillers
  • Supports horses with arthritis and joint issues
  • Reduces inflammation after intense work
  • Promotes comfort and mobility in aging horses
  • No known side effects with natural ingredients
  • Safe for long-term supplementation
  • Supports overall joint health

Usage Instructions

Administer daily mixed with feed. Visible improvements typically occur within 2-3 weeks. For acute conditions, consult your veterinarian regarding increased dosing.

Ideal For

Horses with arthritis, aging horses, performance horses, horses recovering from injury, and any horse experiencing joint pain or inflammation.
